---
title: Fundamental Rights Floor
number: 6
tier: substantive
summary: "Non-negotiable minimum. Rights attach to all persons within jurisdiction, not only citizens."
---

The non-negotiable minimum that subsidiarity operates above. Fundamental rights attach to all persons within the polity's jurisdiction, not only to members or citizens.

Includes democratic participation, bodily autonomy, children's autonomy, freedom from coercion, the right to education, and the right to vote and meaningfully participate in democracy. Robust enforcement that does not depend on the goodwill of local majorities.

The Rights Floor is non-derogable: no emergency, no crisis, and no invocation of national security justifies its suspension. Every authoritarian seizure of power uses emergency as justification; this framework closes that door.
