---
title: Epistemic Autonomy
number: 7
tier: substantive
summary: "Right to truthful information and freedom from systematic manipulation of perception and judgment."
---

The right to access truthful information about matters of public concern and to be free from systematic manipulation of perception and judgment by state or private actors.

This is the 21st-century right that existing constitutional frameworks do not address. Encompasses freedom from propagandistic algorithmic manipulation and the right to encounter reality rather than manufactured simulacra.
