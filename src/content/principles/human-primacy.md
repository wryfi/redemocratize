Human judgment is required over decisions affecting fundamental rights and entitlements. The irreducibility of biological life and human consciousness.

AI and automated systems cannot decide when rights are revoked. This is an ontological claim about what kind of entity has standing to make decisions about human beings' relationship to their polity.
