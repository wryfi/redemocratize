---
title: Commons Governance
number: 11
tier: substantive
summary: "Natural resources, spectrum, public research, data held in trust, governed collectively, not subject to enclosure."
---

Categories of shared wealth — natural resources, the electromagnetic spectrum, public research, data generated by human activity, the atmospheric commons — are held in trust, governed collectively, and cannot be enclosed or privatized.

Governance mechanisms are accountable to the communities that depend on them.
